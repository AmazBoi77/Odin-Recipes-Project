Creamy Dreamy Cheescake

Description

This classic cheesecake recipe offers a delightful balance of creamy richness and a buttery graham cracker crust. The journey begins with a simple yet flavorful crust made by combining crushed graham crackers, a touch of sugar, and melted butter. Once baked to golden perfection, it forms a sturdy foundation for the velvety cheesecake filling. The filling itself is a luscious blend of softened cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and a hint of vanilla extract, resulting in a smooth, decadent texture. After a gentle bake in the oven and a cooling period, the cheesecake sets into a delectable dessert that's both satisfying and customizable. Whether adorned with a fruity compote, a silky chocolate ganache, or a medley of fresh berries, this classic cheesecake promises a slice of pure indulgence for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of crushed graham crackrs
  • 1/4 cups of sugar
  • .5 cups melted salted butter
  • 4 packages of softened cream cheese
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • straw berries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Prepare the crust in a bowl by mixing the grahm crackers and sugar and butter. Mix until well combined
  3. Press the mixture to the borrem of a 9 inch springfoam pan
  4. Bake crust in oven for 10 mins. After baking remove from oven and let cool
  5. In a seperate beat the packages of cream cheese until it becomes creamy
  6. add the brown sugar to the mixture
  7. add one egg at a time to the cream cheese mixture
  8. Add vanilla extract then pour the filling inot the crust
  9. bake the cheesecaje in the oven for 45 mins
  10. afer baking open oven and let cool inside for an hour, then refrigerate
