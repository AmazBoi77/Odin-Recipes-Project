Chilly Billy ColdBrew

Description

If you already have cold brew coffee prepared, creating a mint-themed cold brew is incredibly quick and easy. This refreshing beverage blends the intense richness of cold brew coffee with the invigorating essence of fresh mint, making it a perfect pick-me-up for a hot day. With just a few simple steps, you can enjoy a mint-infused cold brew coffee that's both satisfying and revitalizing.

Ingredients

  • Cold brew 1 cup
  • 6 mint leaves
  • Choice of milk
Instructions
  1. Put 1 cup or more of cold brew in glass and add mint leaves
  2. Place in fridge and let steep for 20-30 minutes
  3. add choice of milk and enjoy
